My Favorite Team: Chicago White Sox

Growing up in the South Loop of Chicago, I have been a White Sox fan for my entire life.

Besides growing up close to where they play, I really fell in love with the team when they won the World Series in 2005.

Unfortunately, since 2005 they haven't been very good. In fact they have been really bad, only making the playoffs once since they won it all in 2005.

However, this year is the year they are supposed to end their rebuild and start contending again in the AL Central for a playoff spot.

I am optimistic, but we'll see what happens.
